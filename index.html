Featured Destinations in Astore

Uncover the unparalleled beauty of Astore, from the vast plains of Deosai to the tranquil waters of Rama Lake. Each location offers a unique story and a lifetime of memories.

Deosai Plains

The "Land of Giants," the world's second-highest alpine plateau, famed for its vibrant wildflowers, unique wildlife, and expansive, breathtaking vistas.

Altitude: ~4,114m Moderate

Sheosar Lake

A jewel of Deosai, this deep blue alpine lake offers stunning reflections of the surrounding peaks and is a sanctuary for diverse flora and fauna.

Altitude: ~4,142m Moderate

Minimarg Valley

A picturesque valley known for its lush green meadows, dense pine forests, and the stunning Rainbow Lake. Offers a tranquil escape with captivating beauty.

Altitude: ~2,800m Challenging (Permit often required)

Rama Valley & Lake

A serene valley with lush meadows, pine forests, and the stunning Rama Lake. It's a gateway to Nanga Parbat's eastern face, offering incredible views.

Altitude: ~3,300m (Lake) Moderate

Tarishing & Rupal Face

The village of Tarishing is the gateway to the awe-inspiring Rupal Face of Nanga Parbat, one of the highest mountain faces in the world. A trekker's dream.

Altitude: ~2,900m (Village) Challenging (Trek)

Rattu Valley

Known for its ski resort in winter and lush landscapes in summer, Rattu offers diverse experiences from adventure sports to peaceful nature walks.

Altitude: ~2,600m Easy to Moderate
